Haller Challenge

Get Started!

Click here to find the guide to Ionic and follow it through the first chapters(1-5), with a twist.

Instead of making a to-do app, you are going to create a small application that allows users to login with twitter and see their last tweets.

The number or amount of tweets is not important, but what is, is being able to see theirs.

This last portion is optional, but will guarantee placement on the project, add a search bar that displays the tweets of any public Twitter user.

Good luck, and have fun! This project has much more potential than most, so show what you can do!
