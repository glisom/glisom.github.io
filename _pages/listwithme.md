# ListWithMe

**The simplest way to share shopping lists in iMessage.**

Create collaborative lists right inside your conversations. No accounts, no sign-ups, no app switching—just open Messages and start making lists together.

[Download on the App Store](#){: .button}

---

## Features

### Real-Time Collaboration
Share lists with anyone in your iMessage conversations. Everyone sees updates as they happen—perfect for shopping together even when you're apart.

### Smart Input
Type naturally. "3 apples" or "milk x2" automatically sets quantities. No extra taps required.

### Stay Organized
- **Categories** — Group items by aisle or type
- **Priorities** — Mark items as low, medium, or high priority
- **Due Dates** — Set deadlines for time-sensitive items

### Quick Actions
- Swipe right to complete items
- Swipe left to delete
- Batch select for bulk actions
- One-tap to clear all completed items

### Activity Feed
See who added, completed, or removed items. Stay in sync with your shopping partners.

### Flexible Sorting
Sort your list by name, category, priority, due date, or keep manual order with drag-to-reorder.

## Privacy First

Your lists are stored on your device and sync via iCloud. We don't collect personal data or analytics. Your lists stay between you and the people you share them with.
