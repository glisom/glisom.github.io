# ListWithMe Support

## Getting Started

ListWithMe is an iMessage app that lets you create and share lists with anyone in your conversations.

### How to Open ListWithMe

1. Open the **Messages** app
2. Start or open a conversation
3. Tap the **+** button (or Apps icon) next to the text field
4. Find and tap **ListWithMe**

### Creating Your First List

1. Tap **New List** and give it a name
2. Tap on the list to open it
3. Type an item name and press return to add it
4. Tap **Send List** to share it in the conversation

## Features

### Smart Quantity Input
Type quantities naturally: "3 apples", "milk x2", or "eggs x12" and ListWithMe will automatically set the item quantity.

### Priorities & Due Dates
Tap the info button (ⓘ) on any item to set priority levels and due dates.

### Categories
Organize items by category (Produce, Dairy, etc.) for easier shopping.

### Sorting & Searching
Use the sort menu to organize by name, category, priority, or due date. Use the search bar to find items quickly.

### Batch Selection
Tap the menu (⋯) and select "Select Items" to choose multiple items for batch complete or delete.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the app on my home screen?**
A: ListWithMe is an iMessage-only app. It lives inside the Messages app and doesn't appear on your home screen.

**Q: Can I use ListWithMe without sending a message?**
A: Yes! You can create and manage lists without sending them. They're stored locally until you choose to share.

**Q: Is my data synced across devices?**
A: Yes, your lists sync via iCloud to all your devices signed into the same Apple ID.

**Q: Can I share lists with Android users?**
A: ListWithMe requires iMessage, so both users need Apple devices.
